# Demo Scenarios

## Scenario 1: International student with flu-like symptoms

- Symptoms: sore throat, fever, fatigue for 3 days
- Insurance: USC Aetna student plan
- Location: USC campus
- Expected outcome: primary care or urgent care recommendation with Mandarin-speaking options

## Scenario 2: New resident with skin rash

- Symptoms: itchy red rash for 1 week
- Insurance: Cigna Open Access
- Location: Koreatown
- Expected outcome: dermatology specialist surfaced with cost and distance explanation

## Scenario 3: Abdominal pain with unclear urgency

- Symptoms: stomach pain after meals, nausea for 2 days
- Insurance: Anthem HMO
- Location: downtown Los Angeles
- Expected outcome: urgent care or internal medicine recommendation with referral note for GI specialist
